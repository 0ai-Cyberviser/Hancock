## 📋 PROJECT SUMMARY

CyberViser is building the first decentralized, AI-powered smart contract auditing platform that makes security accessible, affordable, and community-driven for the entire Ethereum ecosystem.

**Problem:** Traditional audits cost $50k-200k, take weeks, and are centralized bottlenecks.

**Solution:** AI-powered auditing infrastructure combining ML vulnerability detection with community validation, delivering audits at 90% lower cost in 80% less time.
